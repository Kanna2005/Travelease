Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the travel chatbot?
Simply open the chat interface and type your travel question. You can ask about destinations, accommodations, transportation, or anything related to travel in India.
Can the chatbot help with hotel bookings?
While our chatbot doesn't directly handle bookings, it can recommend the best areas to stay, suggest specific hotels based on your budget, and provide links to booking platforms.
Does the chatbot provide real-time information?
Yes, our chatbot can provide real-time information on weather, flight status, and current events. For other information, we regularly update our database to ensure accuracy.
Is the service completely free?
Absolutely! Our travel chatbot is completely free to use. We're here to help promote tourism in India and make travel planning easier for everyone.
